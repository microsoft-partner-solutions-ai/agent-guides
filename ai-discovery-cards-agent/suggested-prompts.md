## Suggested prompts

Copy and paste the following suggested prompts into the "Suggested Prompts" boxes in your agent configuration. Modify as needed.

### Prompt 1:

**Title:** Prep Interview Call

**Message:** I'm going on a initial prep call with partner/customer, what prep-work, ie. interview questions, should I do to make sure we have enough information for the AI Discovery Card workshop.

### Prompt 2:

**Title:** Persona & Journey Map

**Message:** Analyze transcript from our partner/customer workshop and create a key persona and journey map with a few key activities.

### Prompt 3:

**Title:** AI Card Recommendation

**Message:** Based on the scenario, recommend 10 AI Discovery Cards, and a full solution recommendation including Azure and MSFT services, estimated implementation time, and cost.

### Prompt 4:

**Title:** Follow-up Engagement Planning

**Message:** Given this use case and its maturity level, recommend the next engagement type (e.g., PoC, architecture design session, etc) and suggest which SMEs should be included.
